Privacy Policy

1. Information Harvest Parameters

We log minimal telemetry data necessary to optimize local player reservation profiles, tournament scheduling passes, and event notifications. This includes:

  • Identification handles, aliases, and digital routing contacts.
  • Match tournament score submissions inside localized brackets.
  • Transactional logs linked directly to hardware registration booking matrices.

2. Tracking Protocols & Cookies

Our infrastructure deploys core system cookies to hold active authorization tokens and maintain specific UI visual states across page mutations. We do not engage across standard third-party advertising tracking scripts.

3. Protection of Ledger Arrays

Your user profile metrics are enclosed in encrypted databases. Physical terminal logs collected on local match floor arcade hardware are periodically flushed to safeguard individual session logs.
