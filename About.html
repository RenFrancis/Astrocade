About Astrocade

Building the ultimate physical nexus for fostering a welcoming community culture and Japanese arcade engagement.

The Nexus of Gaming Culture

Founded by a small family who met each other in the small town of Mitsuke, Japan. Astrocade is designed to address a critical missing component in contemporary U.K gaming: to establish a cultural family entertainment centre and arcade that demonstrates the player's driven community at it's core.

We reject the standard, uninspired brick-and-mortar storefront layout. Instead, our facility balances deep atmospheric aesthetics with utility. From bright and entertaining arcade asthetics of Japanese arcade culture to responsive audio arrays, every square foot is optimised to celebrate raw hobby expertise. We take pride in listening to our audience and as such are openingly accepting of the communities public ideas for gaming.

Whether you are chasing regional tournament success or simply looking to chill with your local squad or loved ones, Astrocade provides the environment you deserve.
